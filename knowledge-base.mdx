The **Knowledge Base** feature lets you enhance your AI agents with custom, business-specific information so they can answer questions more accurately. Your team can upload documents, add website URLs, or create text entries that agents reference in real time during conversations, ensuring context-aware responses aligned with your processes and policies. This helps reduce hallucinations and makes your agents feel deeply tailored to your brand.

**Note:** This is a premium feature. Upgrade to **Pro** to unlock the full Knowledge Base, including up to 20MB of storage, semantic search for more relevant answers, and unlimited content entries for richer, more comprehensive agent knowledge.
