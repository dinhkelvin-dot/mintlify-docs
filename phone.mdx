<Info>
  **Note**:

  - Notification that phone numbers cost 30 credits per month with automatic charging
  - Numbers are billed monthly and charged automatically.
</Info>

Each number costs **30 credits** per month and can be used for both inbound and outbound calls. Search for numbers by area code, postal code, or city/region to find the perfect local presence for your business.

You can connect each phone number to specific AI agents through the "Manage Connections" feature. Track your active numbers, connection status, and monthly credit usage directly from the dashboard
